Welcome to Atlas

Atlas is a new blast messaging app for iOS and Android that allows you to create a location-based page to message many people at once. People nearby can follow your page to receive messages as a notification, like a text, and can also privately message you. Try it, it's free!









Login











Create a page

Each page is location-based, so it's easy for people to find the pages most relevant to them. For example, if you're a teacher and need to be able to message students and parents, this is an easy way to do that.










Blast message your followers

Once people follow your page, they receive your messages as a notification.










Privately message pages

They can privately reply to your messages, as well as message other nearby page.
